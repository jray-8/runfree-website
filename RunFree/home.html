Why Should I Start Running?

  • Running requires no equipment and can be done anywhere. Just put on some running shoes, head out the door, and you’re ready to go.

  • It can be fun! Running is a very social exercise, and a great way to meet new people or catch up with your family or friends. Nobody said you can’t go running with your buddies! Or if you prefer to run solo, you can zone out to your music or listen to an audio book. This is also a great time to reflect on things or let your mind wander, as you’re alone with your thoughts and nothing else to distract you.

  • Running has several long-term health benefits, both for the body and mind. If you run consistently, you can positively transform your life and progressively shape your body. On the journey, you will become more confident in your abilities and attain an optimistic attitude. And the best part is that the qualities you gain while running can be applied to any other part of your life. And nothing beats the heavenly feeling of relaxing after a long run!

  • It’s exciting! You get to see new things when your running, appreciate nature, and discover new places. Unlike other exercises that keep you in one area, running lets you travel as far as your desire.

  • There’s always a new way to train! You don’t have to run the same way, at the same place each time you go out for a run. That’s the beauty of this exercise. You can run faster or slower, shorter or longer, up hills or around a lake, and you can visit a new location each time. Mixing up your routine is a great way to keep you motivated and make your workout fun.

  • You can enter events such as marathon or races to put your work to the test and meet new people who share your interest. There are also several charities that you can run for to help support a cause you believe in.
